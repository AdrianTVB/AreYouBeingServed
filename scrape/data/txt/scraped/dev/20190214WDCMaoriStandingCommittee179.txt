PUBLIC PARTICIPATION 

A maximum of 30 minutes has been set aside for members of the public to speak on any 
item on the agenda. Up to 5 minutes per person is allowed. As per Standing Order 14.14 
requests to speak must be made to the meeting secretary at least one clear day before 
the meeting; however this requirement may be waived by the Chairperson. 

 

Mr  Pieri  Munro  acknowledged  the  Maori  Standing  Committee  and  introduced  Hawke’s 
Bay Regional Council members. 

 

Mr Rex Graham acknowledged the Committee and also acknowledged Mr Nathan Heath 
as representative for Wairoa.  Look at Ohuia drainage system – what to do about it.  Take 
it to another level to do horticulture and will also be going out to visit Whakaki. 

 

The Chair of HBRC Mr Rex Graham and His Worship the Mayor, Mr C Little talked about 
the  regional  park  –  lighthouse  to  ocean  –  Waitangi  Regional  Park  -  celestial  compass  in 
Napier partnership with hapu and Marae – ancient science on how people travelled in the 
past.  How can a regional park be established in Wairoa and celebrate the history.
