Converting Decorative luminaires to LED luminaires
(Document 19/42)
Councillor
Redstone/Councillor Schollum

the report of the Transportation Asset Manager titled “Converting
Decorative luminaires to LED luminaires” dated 28/02/2019
be received.
B)     That Option C, as outlined in the report in
“A” above, be adopted to replace decorative streetlights to standard LED luminaires by modification of
existing poles where possible.
