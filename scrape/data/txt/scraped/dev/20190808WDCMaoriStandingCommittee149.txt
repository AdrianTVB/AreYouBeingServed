Tuia 250 – Moe Coffey and Paul Ratapu gave a presentation and acknowledged 250 years 
since the first onshore meetings between Maori and European.  Tuia 250 is the national 
commemoration that recognises this milestone in our history. 

 

The  Tuia 250  Voyage  will  start  in  October  2019 on the Tairawhiti  in  Whangaparaoa and 
Turanga, circumnavigate Aotearoa and finish at Mahia in December. 

Mr  N  Heath  from  Hawke’s  Bay  Regional  Council  introduced  Mr  Willie  Smith  (a  retired 
Associate  Professor  in  Geography from  Auckland  University).    Associate Professor  Willie 
Smith is employed as a consultant to undertake a review and investigation into the wider 
Wairoa  communities  concerns  around  the  impacts  of  afforestation  on  community 
wellbeing and the local economy.
